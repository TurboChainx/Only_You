# Privacy Policy — Only You

**Last updated:** March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Only You** mobile application (the “App,” package name `com.onlyyouchat.app`) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

The App is operated by the developer identified on the **Google Play** store listing for Only You (“we,” “us,” or “our”). By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Account and profile information

When you register or use the App, we collect:

- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Name or display name  
- Password (stored using secure hashing; we do not store plain-text passwords)  
- Profile details you choose to provide, including photos if you use camera or gallery features  

### 1.2 Chat and AI interactions

To provide AI-powered conversations, we process:

- Messages you send in chat sessions  
- Session and conversation data needed to show chat history and operate the service  

Your messages are sent to our **backend servers** and may be transmitted to **OpenAI** (or another AI provider we configure) to generate responses. OpenAI’s handling of data is described in their privacy policy: [https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy).

### 1.3 Device and technical data

We may collect limited technical information such as:

- Device type and operating system version  
- App version  
- Diagnostic or error data needed to fix bugs and protect the service  

### 1.4 Permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions, depending on your device and features used:

- **Camera** and **photos** — profile images and related features  
- **Microphone** — if voice-related features are enabled  
- **SMS** (read/receive), **phone**, **contacts** — only where used for verification, calling, or related features described in the App; you can revoke these in system settings  

---

## 2. How we use information

We use the information above to:

- Create and manage your account  
- Provide AI chat and character features  
- Store chat history as implemented in the product  
- Operate, secure, and improve the App and backend  
- Comply with applicable law  

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 3. Where data is stored and transferred

- **Account, chat, and app data** are stored on servers that run our **backend API** and **MongoDB** database (hosted on infrastructure we operate or control, for example a cloud VPS or similar host).  
- Data may be processed in the United States or other regions where OpenAI and our hosting providers operate.  
- If you are in the EEA, UK, or other regions with privacy laws, you may have additional rights; contact us as described in Section 7.
